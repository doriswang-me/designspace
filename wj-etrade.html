WJ-Etrade

B2C e-commerce redesign using UX research + data analysis to improve conversion and user experience.

Overview

WJ-Etrade is an outdoor furniture cover platform. The redesign focused on solving high drop-off between search and purchase.

Problem

Users struggled to find products and understand value, leading to low conversion rates.

Solution

Redesigned navigation, added product customization, and improved product detail pages.

Results

+15% engagement · +20% session duration · +18% AOV
